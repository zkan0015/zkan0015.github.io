Perceived Stress Scale(PSS) Calculator

This stress assessment employs the Perceived Stress Scale (PSS), initially developed by Cohen et al. in 1983. This assessment is extensively acknowledged for evaluating the perception of stress and holds clinical validation. It is widely adopted by esteemed medical institutions like the NHS and other reputable healthcare services.
